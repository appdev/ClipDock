ClipShelf

把复制过的文本、链接、图片、文件和颜色保存在一条贴近工作流的底部内容架里。需要时呼出，确认后复用，然后继续当前任务。 Keep copied text, links, images, files, and colors in a shelf that stays close to your Mac workflow. Open it when needed, confirm what you want, then get back to work.

核心功能 Core features

剪贴板历史，应该像系统能力一样自然。 Clipboard history should feel like a native system ability.

ClipShelf 聚焦高频复制粘贴场景，不把简单工具做成复杂后台。所有入口都围绕快速浏览、确认和复用展开。 ClipShelf stays focused on the repeat copy and paste moments. Every surface is built around scanning, confirming, and reusing content quickly.

01

全局呼出Open from anywhere

按下 Command + Shift + V，从屏幕底部呼出内容架，不打断当前应用上下文。Press Command + Shift + V to bring up the shelf from the bottom of the screen without leaving your current app.

02

类型化卡片Typed item cards

文本、链接、颜色、图片和文件各自使用更适合识别的展示方式，降低误选成本。Text, links, colors, images, and files use distinct card presentations so you can recognize the right item faster.

03

Pinboard 整理Pinboard organization

把常用片段固定到 Pinboard，让长期资料和临时剪贴板记录分开管理。Save reusable material into Pinboards so long-lived snippets stay separate from transient clipboard activity.

04

隐私规则Privacy controls

当前版本数据保存在本机，可通过规则排除敏感来源应用，日常使用不需要账号。History stays on your Mac in the current version, sensitive apps can be excluded, and normal use does not require an account.

使用方式 Workflow

复制很多次，也不怕覆盖最后一次。 Copy many times without losing the last useful thing.

ClipShelf 不要求你改变习惯。继续复制，需要复用时再打开内容架。 ClipShelf does not ask you to change habits. Keep copying, then open the shelf when you need something again.

1

继续复制Keep copying

ClipShelf 在后台记录受支持的剪贴板内容，包括文字、链接、颜色、图片和文件。ClipShelf records supported clipboard content in the background, including text, links, colors, images, and files.

2

快速定位Find the item

使用横向卡片、搜索、类型筛选或 Pinboard 标签，快速定位需要复用的内容。Use horizontal cards, search, type filters, or Pinboard labels to find the content you need.

3

确认后复用Preview and reuse

先预览再放回系统剪贴板，减少误粘贴，然后回到原来的应用继续工作。Preview before sending it back to the system clipboard, then return to the app you were using.

设置与隐私 Preferences and privacy

把敏感边界放在用户可见的位置。 Keep sensitive boundaries visible and adjustable.

偏好设置拆分为通用、隐私、快捷键和关于页面。隐私规则可以排除指定来源，快捷键可以配合个人工作流调整。 Preferences are split into General, Privacy, Shortcuts, and About pages. Privacy rules can exclude selected sources, and shortcuts can fit your workflow.

01

本地记录Local history

当前版本的剪贴板历史保存在你的 Mac 上，日常使用不需要登录账号。Clipboard history stays on your Mac in the current version, with no account required for everyday use.

02

来源排除Source exclusions

03

快捷键Shortcuts

保留可调整快捷键，但页面不再把设置窗口作为主展示内容。Shortcuts remain adjustable, while the page keeps preferences out of the main visual focus.
